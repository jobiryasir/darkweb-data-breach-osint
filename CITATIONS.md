# References & Citations

This document tracks the sources, methodologies, and datasets utilized in the development and research conducted within this repository.

## 📚 Core Methodologies & Frameworks
*   **OSINT Framework:** [osintframework.com](https://osintframework.com/) - Essential methodologies for information gathering.
*   **The Privacy Guides:** [privacyguides.org](https://privacyguides.org/) - Standards for OPSEC and digital anonymity.
*   **Cyber Forensics Standards:** References from established digital forensics research papers and NIST cybersecurity guidelines.

## 🌐 Research Platforms
*   **Tor Project:** [torproject.org](https://www.torproject.org/) - Infrastructure research and network analysis.
*   **Blockchain Explorers:** [Etherscan](https://etherscan.io/), [Blockchain.com](https://www.blockchain.com/) - Data points used for crypto auditing/intelligence.

## 🛠️ Tools & Libraries
*   **Hunchly:** Documentation and methodology for digital investigation.
*   **OnionScan:** Research reports on deep web infrastructure analysis.

## 📝 Guidelines for Contributors
If you are contributing research data, please ensure that you provide credible sources. All citations should be formatted using the following structure:
*   **Title/Resource Name:** [Link]
*   **Category:** (e.g., OSINT, OPSEC, Forensics)
*   **Description:** A brief note on how this source was utilized.
