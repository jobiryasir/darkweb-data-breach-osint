# OSINT Garlic Research Toolkit

![RESEARCH](https://img.shields.io/badge/RESEARCH-ACTIVE-yellow?style=flat-square) ![OSINT](https://img.shields.io/badge/OSINT-COLLECTION-orange?style=flat-square)

This file is a compilation of research projects based on OSINT (Open Source Intelligence) and Garlic Routing (anonymity network). It is used for information gathering, network privacy, and digital forensic research.

| Resource Name | Focus Area | Access Link |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **OSINT Framework** | Map of OSINT tools and methodologies | [Visit Site](https://osintframework.com/) |
| **IntelTechniques** | OSINT and privacy tools by Michael Bazzell | [Visit Site](https://inteltechniques.com/) |

---

### 💡 Research Objectives
* **OSINT Integration:** Proper searching and verification of publicly available information.
* **Garlic Methodology:** Understanding network encryption and traffic analysis through Garlic Routing.
* **Privacy Research:** Analyzing anonymous communication protocols and their security.
