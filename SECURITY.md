# Security Policy

This security policy outlines the standards maintained within this repository and provides instructions for reporting potential vulnerabilities.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

I take the security of my research and the content in this repository seriously. If you believe you have found a security vulnerability or a privacy concern related to the content hosted here, please report it privately.

**How to report:**
*   **Contact:** Please reach out to me directly via [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/jobiryasir07).
*   **Details:** Please include a detailed description of the vulnerability, steps to reproduce it, and any relevant information that might help in mitigating the issue.

## Security Scope
This repository focuses on:
*   **Research:** Deep/Dark web infrastructure, OSINT methodologies, and digital forensics.
*   **Privacy:** Promoting best practices for digital anonymity and OPSEC.
*   **Education:** Providing structured intelligence for defensive and ethical research.

## Our Pledge
*   **Responsible Disclosure:** I am committed to addressing all reported vulnerabilities in a timely and responsible manner.
*   **No Malicious Intent:** All research shared here is for educational and defensive purposes only.
*   **Data Integrity:** I strive to ensure that all information and tools referenced are used in accordance with ethical standards and legal guidelines.

## Safety Guidelines for Users
*   Do not use the information provided in this repository to perform illegal activities or unauthorized access to any system.
*   Always ensure your own environment is secure when conducting research or analyzing network data.
*   When in doubt, prioritize your own digital safety and anonymity.
