## 🛡️ Search OpSec Parameters

*   **Avoid Trailing Footprints:** When using clearnet gateways (like Bullmask, Ahmia, or OnionLand) to browse dark web links, never click directly on a `.onion` link from a standard browser; always copy the link addresses manually into your hardened Tor Browser or I2P router environment.
*   **Query Compartmentalization:** Do not search for your personal handles, real-world locations, or unique pseudonyms inside deep-web engines, as crawl engines often log historical string correlations.
*   **Metadata Leak Warning:** Be cautious when downloading indexed images or attachments directly through aggregated search interfaces.
