### ⛓️ Operational Guidelines

* **Network Topology:** Analyze node connectivity patterns and backend clustering to map the threat landscape.
* **Architecture:** Leverage the onion routing mechanism understanding to anticipate circuit shifts.
* **De-cloaking:** Use DNS history, SSL fingerprinting, and application-level triggers to verify origin servers.

---

### 🔍 Operational Readiness Checklist

- [ ] **Clean State Snapshot:** A fresh, uninfected state snapshot of your investigation VM is saved.
- [ ] **Egress Filter:** Ensure all traffic is routed through a secondary proxy to mask research origins.
- [ ] **No Personal Attribution:** No clearnet accounts, passwords, or real-world pseudonyms in the sandbox.
- [ ] **Data Sanitization:** Remove tracking pixels or canary tokens from harvested data before transfer.
- [ ] **URL Verification:** Cross-verify all .onion links against cryptographically signed sources.
