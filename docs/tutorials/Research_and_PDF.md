### ⛓️ Research Handling Guidelines

* **Environment Integrity:** Always download and open PDFs within an isolated, air-gapped, or sandboxed environment (Whonix/Tails) to prevent potential macro-based exploits or hidden script execution.
* **Metadata Stripping:** Before importing these files into your primary knowledge base, use tools (like `exiftool`) to strip all document metadata, author tags, and embedded thumbnails.
* **Persistence:** Save all research materials to an encrypted, offline-only container. Do not store these files on cloud-synchronized drives.

---

### 🔍 Research Integrity Checklist

- [ ] **Isolated Reader:** Using a secure, hardened PDF viewer (e.g., QPDFView or a sandbox-isolated environment).
- [ ] **JavaScript Disabled:** Ensure the PDF reader has JavaScript execution capabilities permanently disabled.
- [ ] **Checksum Verification:** Verify the SHA-256 hash of all downloaded documents against trusted source fingerprints.
- [ ] **Metadata Audit:** All downloaded files have been scrubbed of origin metadata.
