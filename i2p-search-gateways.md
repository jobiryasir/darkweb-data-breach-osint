## 🛡️ Critical I2P OpSec

*   **Configure Proxy:** Ensure your browser is configured to use the I2P HTTP Proxy (port 4444).
*   **Do Not Use Public Proxies:** Never use a clearnet-to-I2P gateway. These bypass the entire point of I2P's internal routing and destroy your anonymity.
*   **Anonymity:** Avoid using the same browser profile for both clearnet browsing and I2P browsing.
